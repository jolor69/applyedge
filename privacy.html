Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

This Privacy Policy explains what information ApplyEdge (operated by NeuLab Inc.) collects, how we use it, and who we share it with.

1. What We Collect

  • Account information: your email address and a hashed password.
  • Content you submit: resume text, job descriptions, and template text you enter into our tools.
  • Usage data: which tools you use, how many scans you've used this billing period, and plan/subscription status.
  • Payment information: handled directly by PayPal — we do not store your card or bank details.

2. How We Use It

We use your content solely to generate the outputs you request — an ATS score, a curated resume, a cover letter, a Signal one-pager, a Cold Read review, or Job Match search results. We do not sell your resume content, and we do not use it to train AI models.

3. Third-Party Processors

To provide the service, your content passes through the following third parties:

ProviderPurpose
OpenRouter / DeepSeekAI processing for resume, cover letter, Cold Read, and Signal generation
JoobleJob listing search (all plans)
Glints (via Apify)Job listing search (Seeker and Campaigner plans)
PayPalSubscription and one-time payment processing
ResendTransactional email (verification, password reset, receipts)
CloudflareHosting, database, and infrastructure

Each provider processes only what's needed to perform their function and is bound by their own privacy terms.

4. Data Retention

Your resume and job description inputs are not stored beyond what's needed to generate your result, except: Seeker and Campaigner plan users can opt to save outputs to account history, which is retained until you delete it or close your account. Your login session is stored as a token in your browser's local storage.

5. Your Rights

You can request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data at any time by emailing ai.neulab.inc@gmail.com. You can also delete your account directly, which removes your stored history and account information.

6. Cookies and Local Storage

ApplyEdge uses browser local storage to keep you signed in. We do not use third-party advertising or tracking cookies.
